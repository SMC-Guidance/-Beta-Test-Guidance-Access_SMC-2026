Getting Started

How to set up and use the Guidance Center website

  1. Get your registration codeAsk the Guidance Office for the one-time registration code. You will need it to create your account.
  2. Create your accountOn the Register tab, enter your full name, a username (letters, numbers and underscores), a password (at least 8 characters with 1 uppercase letter and 1 number), and your registration code. Then click Create Account.
  3. Sign inSwitch to the Sign In tab and log in with your new username and password.
  4. Explore the DashboardSee session and student statistics at a glance, and use the Session Notes Google Form button to record a new counseling session.
  5. Review student recordsUse the search bar and the dashboard tables to filter and review the sessions assigned to you.
  6. Teacher Evaluations & ResultsTrack teacher evaluations and open Evaluation Results to compute and view processed scores.
  7. Set up your profileClick your name in the top-right corner, then choose My Profile to add your photo, role and notes.
  8. Sign out safelyWhen you are done, open the same top-right menu and click Sign Out.

🔒Why this site is hacker-proof

Every student record is protected by several independent layers of security:

  • 🔑
    Passwords are never storedWe keep only a scrambled one-way fingerprint of your password — HMAC-SHA256 with a unique salt and a secret server pepper, stretched 12,000 times. Nobody, not even an administrator, can read your real password.
  • 🎫
    Tamper-proof sign-in tokensEach session uses a digitally signed token that expires automatically and is verified with a timing-attack-safe check, so tokens cannot be forged or replayed.
  • 🚨
    Brute-force lockoutToo many wrong password attempts automatically lock the entire site until an administrator restores it with a secret unlock code.
  • 🛡️
    Role-based access controlEvery action is re-checked on the server. Counselors, co-admins and admins can only reach the data and tools their role allows.
  • 🧼
    Input is sanitisedAll text is cleaned before it is displayed, blocking cross-site scripting (XSS) and injection attacks.
  • 🔐
    Encrypted & Google-securedAll traffic travels over encrypted HTTPS/TLS, and records live inside Google's protected infrastructure.
